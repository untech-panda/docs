## How customers add products with Revize

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse available products">
    From the Revize Customer Portal, customers click **Add products** to see your curated product selection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select items to add">
    Choose products with variants (size, color, quantity) using the familiar product browser interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review order changes">
    See new items added to their order with updated totals and shipping information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay for additions">
    Click **Pay now** to charge the additional amount and confirm the expanded order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  With Revize's rules engine, merchants can choose which products to show under "Add products" based on order and product tags. This customization is currently available through our support team - reach out (hello@revize.app) with your customization request.
</Tip>

## Configure product additions in Revize

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the feature">
    In your **Revize Order Editing** settings, turn on **Add products** under Customer edit options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose available products">
    Use Revize's powerful rules engine to select which products customers can add. Contact support for custom product selection based on order contents, tags, or collections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the experience">
    Place a test order and try adding products to ensure the flow works smoothly for your customers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do customers add products to their orders with Revize?" icon="cart-shopping">
