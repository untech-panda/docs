## How customers add products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse available products">
    From the Customer Portal, customer clicks **Add products** to see your curated product selection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select items to add">
    Choose products with variants (size, color, quantity) using the familiar product browser interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review order changes">
    See new items added to their order with updated totals and shipping information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay for additions">
    Click **Pay now** to charge the additional amount and confirm the expanded order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure product additions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the feature">
    In **Order Editing** settings, turn on **Add products** under Customer edit options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose available products">
    Select which products customers can add - all products, specific collections, or curated lists.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up recommendations">
    Configure product recommendation rules based on order contents, customer behavior, or manual curation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the flow">
    Place a test order and try adding products to ensure the experience works smoothly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
