## How customers edit shipping addresses

<Steps>
  <Step title="Spot the address mistake">
    Customer notices a wrong shipping address on the thank you page or Shopify order status page and clicks **Edit shipping address**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the shipping address">
    Change street address, apartment number, city, state, ZIP code, or country in the address form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic recalculations">
    <Tip>
      Revize automatically recalculates taxes and shipping rates based on the new address location.
    </Tip>
    
    When the address changes, order totals update instantly to reflect:
    - New sales tax rates for the destination
    - Updated shipping costs based on distance/zone
    - International duties and fees (if applicable)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify and save changes">
    Customer reviews tax/shipping changes and clicks **Confirm** to apply the address update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order updates instantly">
    The shipping address updates immediately in Shopify and syncs to your fulfillment system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Smart automatic recalculations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Tax recalculation" icon="receipt">
    **Instant tax updates**
    
    When customers change their shipping address, Revize automatically recalculates taxes based on the new location:
    
    - State and local sales tax rates
    - Tax-exempt regions and zones
    - Digital tax requirements by jurisdiction
    
    **Example:** Customer changes address from Oregon (no sales tax) to California (7.25% sales tax) - order total updates instantly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Shipping rate updates" icon="truck">
    **Real-time shipping calculations**
    
    Shipping rates automatically update when address or cart contents change:
    
    - Distance-based shipping zones
    - Regional carrier availability
    - Express vs standard options by location
    - International shipping restrictions
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Multiple calculation triggers" icon="calculator">
    **When recalculations happen**
    
    Revize recalculates taxes and shipping when customers:
    
    - Change shipping address to different state/country
    - Add or remove items from their order
    - Modify product quantities
    - Upgrade or downgrade shipping methods
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Shopify address editing scenarios

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Typos & Autocomplete Errors">
    **Most common address mistakes:**
    - Misspelled street names from mobile autocomplete
    - Wrong house or apartment numbers  
    - Missing or incorrect ZIP codes
    
    **Before/After example:**
    - ❌ `123 Main Stret, Apt A, 90210`
    - ✅ `123 Main Street, Apt 2A, 90211`
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Missing Address Details">
    **What customers frequently add:**
    - Apartment, unit, or suite numbers
    - Building names or floor numbers
    - Gate codes or special delivery instructions
    
    **Before/After example:**
    - ❌ `456 Oak Street, Chicago IL`
    - ✅ `456 Oak Street, Unit 2B, Chicago IL 60601`
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Wrong Shipping Location">
    **Common location mix-ups:**
    - Home address instead of work address
    - Old address from Shopify saved addresses
    - Wrong city, state, or ZIP code
    
    **Before/After example:**
    - ❌ `Home address: 789 Pine Ave, Chicago IL`
    - ✅ `Work address: 321 Oak St, New York NY`
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Address Changes & Moves">
    **Life events requiring updates:**
    - Recent moves to new addresses
    - Seasonal address changes
    - Vacation or temporary shipping addresses
    - Business relocations
    
    **Before/After example:**
    - ❌ `Old apartment: 555 Elm St, Austin TX`
    - ✅ `New house: 777 Maple Dr, Dallas TX`
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Enable shipping address editing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Order Editing settings">
    In the Revize app, click **Order Editing** in the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable shipping address edits">
    In **Customer edit options**, toggle on **Shipping address** and click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Address editing goes live">
    The shipping address editing option appears in the Customer Portal for all new orders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Shipping address editing with automatic tax and shipping recalculation is essential for preventing delivery failures. Enable it to reduce support tickets, save shipping costs, and improve customer satisfaction.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do address changes affect order pricing?" icon="dollar-sign">
    Yes, Revize automatically recalculates taxes and shipping rates when customers change their shipping address. Customers see any price changes before confirming the edit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can customers edit international shipping addresses?" icon="globe">
    Absolutely. Address editing works for international orders with proper validation for each country's postal format and automatic tax/duty recalculation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if the new address has higher shipping costs?" icon="truck">
    Customers see the new shipping rate immediately and must pay any difference before the address change is confirmed. The payment flows through Shopify's secure checkout.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does address editing integrate with my 3PL?" icon="warehouse">
    Address changes sync automatically to your fulfillment system when orders are released from hold. Since orders remain on hold during the edit window, your 3PL receives the corrected address without any manual intervention.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
