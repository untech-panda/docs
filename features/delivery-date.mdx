## How customers set delivery dates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select delivery date option">
    From the Customer Portal, customer clicks **Delivery date** to access date scheduling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose preferred date">
    Pick from available delivery dates based on shipping method and location constraints.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review timing impact">
    See how the selected date affects shipping method and any additional costs involved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm delivery schedule">
    Click **Confirm** to set the delivery date and update the order shipping timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enable delivery date selection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the feature">
    In **Order Editing** settings, turn on **Delivery date** under Customer edit options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure date constraints">
    Set minimum advance notice, maximum delivery window, and carrier-specific limitations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up carrier integration">
    Ensure your shipping integrations support delivery date requests and scheduling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test various scenarios">
    Verify delivery date selection works with different shipping methods and destinations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
