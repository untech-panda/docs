## What Customers Can Edit

### Shipping Information

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Shipping Address Updates" icon="truck">
**Customers can modify:**
- Street address and apartment numbers
- City, state, and postal code
- Country (with shipping zone validation)
- Address type (residential/commercial)

**Limitations:**
- Must be within the same shipping zone (unless configured otherwise)
- Address changes may affect shipping costs
- Some carriers may have restrictions on late address changes
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Shipping Method Changes" icon="shipping-fast">
**Available options:**
- Upgrade to faster shipping (if available)
- Downgrade to slower shipping (with refund)
- Change carrier preferences
- Modify delivery instructions

**Note:** Shipping method changes will automatically recalculate costs and process refunds/charges as needed.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Product Modifications

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Quantity Adjustments" icon="plus-minus">
**Increase quantities:**
- Additional payment processed automatically
- Inventory checked in real-time
- Shipping costs recalculated if needed

**Decrease quantities:**
- Automatic refunds for removed items
- Shipping costs adjusted accordingly
- Inventory returned to available stock
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Variant Changes" icon="palette">
**Customers can swap:**
- Colors and sizes
- Material options
- Style variations
- Product configurations

**Requirements:**
- Same base product family
- Variant must be in stock
- Price differences handled automatically
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Add New Items" icon="plus">
**Adding products:**
- Browse available products
- Add items to existing order
- Automatic payment processing
- Combined shipping optimization

**Limitations:**
- Must be from the same store
- Subject to inventory availability
- May affect shipping costs
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Remove Items" icon="minus">
**Removing products:**
- Instant refunds processed
- Shipping costs recalculated
- Inventory automatically updated
- Order total adjusted

**Note:** Minimum order requirements may apply for some stores.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Payment & Billing

<Info>
All payment adjustments are processed automatically. Customers don't need to re-enter payment information for most changes.
</Info>

**Automatic Processing:**
- **Price Increases**: Additional charges to original payment method
- **Price Decreases**: Refunds to original payment method
- **Payment Failures**: Customers notified and given alternative options
- **Partial Refunds**: Processed within 3-5 business days

## Edit Time Windows

<Warning>
Edit windows are configurable per store. Default settings may vary.
</Warning>

### Standard Time Limits
- **Default Window**: 30 minutes after order placement
- **Extended Window**: Up to 24 hours (configurable)
- **Rush Orders**: May have shortened windows
- **Custom Settings**: Can vary by customer segment or product type

### Window Extensions
Customers can request extensions in special circumstances:
- Technical issues during editing
- Need to verify shipping information
- Consultation with gift recipients
- Product availability questions

## Customer Experience Flow

<Steps>
<Step title="Order Placement">
Customer completes checkout and receives order confirmation with edit link.
</Step>

<Step title="Edit Access">
Customer clicks edit link in email or accesses through customer account portal.
</Step>

<Step title="Make Changes">
Customer reviews order and makes desired modifications using intuitive interface.
</Step>

<Step title="Review & Confirm">
Customer reviews all changes, payment adjustments, and confirms modifications.
</Step>

<Step title="Processing">
Changes are applied automatically, payments processed, and updated confirmation sent.
</Step>
</Steps>
