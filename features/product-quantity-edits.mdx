## What customers can change

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Variants">
    **Size, color, style changes**
    
    - Switch from Medium to Large  
    - Change from Blue to Red
    - Swap Regular to Premium version
    - Update from 32GB to 64GB
    
    *Revize handles price differences automatically*
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Quantities">
    **Increase or decrease amounts**
    
    - Change from 2 items to 3 items
    - Reduce quantity from 5 to 1
    - Add extra units for bulk discounts
    
    *Perfect for bulk orders or gift sets*
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Product Swaps">
    **Replace with different products**
    
    - Swap iPhone case for Samsung case
    - Change from t-shirt to hoodie
    - Replace sold-out item with alternative
    
    *Great for seasonal or trend changes*
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Remove Items">
    **Delete products from order**
    
    - Remove duplicate items
    - Delete unwanted add-ons
    - Clear accidental clicks
    
    *Automatic refunds for removed items*
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How customers make changes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select item to change">
    From the Customer Portal, customer clicks **Edit** next to any product in their order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose new variant or quantity">
    Pick different size, color, quantity, or browse alternative products in a clean selection interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review price difference">
    See exactly how the change affects the order total with clear before/after pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the change">
    Click **Pay now** (for increases), **Refund** (for decreases), or **Confirm** (same price).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment handling

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Price increases" icon="credit-card">
    **When new items cost more**
    
    Customer sees a **Pay now** card with:
    - Exact additional amount to charge
    - Updated order total
    - Payment method (saved card or new payment)
    
    Payment processes automatically through Shopify's secure checkout.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Price decreases" icon="money-bill">
    **When new items cost less**
    
    Customer sees a **Refund** card with:
    - Amount being refunded
    - Refund destination (original payment or store credit)
    - Updated order total
    
    Refunds process according to your [payment settings](/setup/payments-and-refunds).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Same price changes" icon="equals">
    **When totals don't change**
    
    Customer sees a **Confirm** button for:
    - Variant swaps at the same price
    - Quantity changes that cancel out
    - Product swaps with matching costs
    
    No payment processing needed - just order updates.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Inventory and availability

<Steps>
  <Step title="Real-time stock checking">
    Revize checks current inventory levels when customers browse alternatives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear availability status">
    Products show as **In Stock**, **Low Stock**, or **Out of Stock** with exact quantities when helpful.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Alternative suggestions">
    When items are unavailable, customers see similar products that are in stock.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Backorder handling">
    For backorderable items, customers see expected availability dates and can choose to wait or swap.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enable product editing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Order Editing settings">
    In the Revize app, click **Order Editing** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable product and quantity changes">
    In **Customer edit options**, turn on **Product variants and quantity** and click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure restrictions (optional)">
    Set limits on quantity increases, variant swaps, or specific product categories if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
