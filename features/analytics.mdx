## Operational efficiency metrics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Support ticket reduction" icon="ticket-alt">
    **Measuring support impact**
    
    Compare support volume before and after implementing order editing:
    - Percentage reduction in order-related tickets
    - Types of tickets most affected
    - Support team time savings
    - Customer satisfaction with self-service
    
    **Typical results:** 40-60% reduction in order change requests
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Fulfillment improvements" icon="boxes">
    **Warehouse and shipping efficiency**
    
    Track operational improvements:
    - Fewer wrong-address shipments
    - Reduced pick errors from last-minute changes
    - Lower return processing volume
    - Improved inventory accuracy
    
    **Cost savings:** Significant reduction in fulfillment rework and corrections
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payment processing efficiency" icon="credit-card">
    **Financial operations impact**
    
    Monitor payment-related improvements:
    - Reduced manual refund processing
    - Fewer payment disputes and chargebacks
    - Automated payment collection for upsells
    - Improved cash flow from reduced cancellations
    
    **Financial benefits:** Streamlined payment operations and reduced processing costs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
