## How customers edit invoices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access invoice editing">
    From the Customer Portal, customer clicks **Edit tax invoice** to open the invoice editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update billing information">
    Change company name, billing address, tax IDs, or other required business details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview updated invoice">
    See exactly how the corrected invoice will look with all updated information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and download">
    Click **Update invoice** to save changes and download the corrected PDF invoice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common invoice corrections

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Wrong company name" icon="building">
    **Legal entity corrections**
    
    - Subsidiary vs parent company
    - Merged or acquired company names
    - DBA vs legal name issues
    - International entity variations
    
    **Example:** Order placed by "ABC Corp" but invoice needs "ABC Corporation Ltd."
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Missing tax IDs" icon="hashtag">
    **Tax registration numbers**
    
    - Forgot to enter VAT number at checkout
    - New tax registration obtained
    - Different tax ID for different divisions
    - Seasonal business registration changes
    
    **Example:** EU business needs VAT number added for proper VAT treatment
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Billing address errors" icon="map-marker-alt">
    **Corporate address issues**
    
    - Headquarters vs billing department address
    - Recent office relocations
    - International billing requirements
    - Department-specific billing needs
    
    **Example:** Invoice needs accounts payable department address, not main office
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Purchase order numbers" icon="clipboard-list">
    **Procurement tracking**
    
    - PO number required for payment processing
    - Department budget allocation codes
    - Project-specific cost tracking
    - Vendor payment system requirements
    
    **Example:** Corporate policy requires PO number on all invoices for payment
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Enable invoice editing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the feature">
    In **Order Editing** settings, turn on **Invoice and tax info** under Customer edit options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure tax validation">
    Set up tax ID validation for your target markets (VAT, GST, EIN, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize invoice template">
    Ensure invoice layout includes all required fields for your business customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test with B2B orders">
    Place test orders and verify invoice editing works correctly with various tax scenarios.
  </Step>
</Steps>
