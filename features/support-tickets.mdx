## How customers request support

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access support option">
    From the Customer Portal, customer clicks **Contact Support** to open a support request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe their issue">
    Fill out a simple form explaining what they need help with, with order context pre-filled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose contact preference">
    Select preferred communication method: email, phone callback, or live chat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit request">
    Click **Send Request** to create a support ticket with full order history attached.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customer experience design

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Seamless integration" icon="puzzle-piece">
    **Feels like part of the portal**
    
    Support requests open within the same interface customers are already using. No new tabs, no separate login, no context switching.
    
    **Benefits:**
    - Maintains customer focus
    - Reduces abandonment
    - Keeps order context visible
    - Streamlines the help process
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Clear expectations" icon="clock">
    **Response time transparency**
    
    Customers see exactly when to expect a response:
    - "We'll respond within 2 hours during business hours"
    - "Expect a callback within 30 minutes"
    - "Live chat available now"
    
    **Benefits:**
    - Sets realistic expectations
    - Reduces repeat requests
    - Builds trust in support process
    - Allows customers to plan accordingly
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Multiple contact options" icon="comments">
    **Customer choice in communication**
    
    Different customers prefer different support channels:
    - Email for non-urgent, detailed issues
    - Phone for complex problems needing discussion
    - Chat for quick questions and real-time help
    - Video call for technical demonstration needs
    
    **Benefits:**
    - Accommodates communication preferences
    - Increases customer satisfaction
    - Improves resolution efficiency
    - Reduces channel switching
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Status tracking" icon="list-check">
    **Ticket progress visibility**
    
    Customers can see their support request status:

    - "Response sent - please check email"
    - "Resolved - please confirm satisfaction"
    
    **Benefits:**
    - Reduces "where is my response" calls
    - Provides peace of mind
    - Allows for better planning
    - Improves overall experience
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Enable support tickets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the feature">
    In **Order Editing** settings, turn on **Support tickets** under Customer edit options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure support routing">
    Set up ticket categories and routing rules to direct requests to appropriate team members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate support systems">
    Connect with your existing help desk or CRM to ensure tickets flow into your support workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set response expectations">
    Configure automatic replies with realistic response timeframes based on your support capacity.
  </Step>
</Steps>
