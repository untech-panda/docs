## How customers upgrade shipping

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access upgrade options">
    From the Revize Customer Portal, customers click **Upgrade shipping method** to see faster delivery options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare shipping methods">
    View all available shipping methods with delivery dates, costs, and service descriptions from your store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select faster delivery">
    Choose from express, overnight, or other expedited options based on your Shopify shipping configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay the difference">
    Click **Pay now** to charge the shipping upgrade fee through Shopify's secure checkout system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instant order update">
    Shipping method updates immediately in Shopify and syncs to your fulfillment system with the new service level.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Smart shipping integration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Works with your existing shipping setup" icon="truck">
    **Seamless integration with popular shipping solutions**
    
    Revize automatically pulls from your configured shipping methods:
    
    - **Shopify Shipping** - built-in carrier rates
    - **Advanced Shipping Rules** - complex rate calculations
    - **Bespoke Shipping** - custom shipping configurations  
    - **Carrier-connected rates** - UPS, FedEx, DHL, USPS live rates
    - **Third-party shipping apps** - ShipperHQ, Parcelify, Advanced Shipping Manager
    
    <Note>
      Using carrier rates or custom shipping apps? Contact us and we'll configure Revize to work seamlessly with your setup.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Dynamic rate calculations" icon="calculator">
    **Shipping rates update automatically**
    
    Shipping options and pricing adjust in real-time when customers:
    
    - **Change shipping address** - rates recalculate for new delivery location
    - **Add or remove items** - shipping costs adjust for cart weight and size
    - **Modify quantities** - carrier rates update for package dimensions
    - **Qualify for free shipping** - automatic free shipping when thresholds are met
    
    Customers always see accurate, current shipping options and pricing.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Flexible fulfillment options" icon="boxes-stacked">
    **Configure shipping to match your workflow**
    
    Revize supports different shipping approaches:
    
    - **Combined shipping (default)** - all items ship together with unified rates
    - **Split shipping** - items can ship separately with individual rates per package
    
    If your store has split shipping configured, just let us know and we'll enable it in one minute for your store.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  **Smart upgrade detection:** When customers add products and their order value exceeds your free shipping threshold, Revize automatically offers free shipping as an upgrade option (if enabled in your store settings).
</Tip>

## Enable shipping upgrades

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Order Editing settings">
    In the Revize app, navigate to **Order Editing** in the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the shipping upgrade feature">
    Under **Customer edit options**, toggle on **Upgrade shipping method** and click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the experience">
    Place a test order and verify that shipping upgrade options appear correctly in the Customer Portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do customers upgrade their shipping method in Revize?" icon="truck">
    Customers click "Upgrade shipping method" in their order portal, compare available faster delivery options, see the exact cost difference, and pay the upgrade fee through Shopify's secure payment system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What shipping methods can customers choose from?" icon="route">
    Customers can select from any shipping methods configured in your Shopify store, including carrier rates, shipping app configurations, and custom shipping rules you've set up.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do shipping upgrades work for international orders?" icon="globe">
    Yes, shipping upgrades are location-aware and only display services available to each customer's specific delivery address, including international shipping options and restrictions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are shipping upgrade payments processed?" icon="credit-card">
    Upgrade fees are charged through Shopify's payment system using the customer's saved payment method. Customers see the exact additional cost before confirming the upgrade.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you integrate with shipping apps like Advanced Shipping Rules?" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Absolutely. Revize works with popular shipping apps including Advanced Shipping Rules, Bespoke Shipping, ShipperHQ, Parcelify, and Advanced Shipping Manager. Contact us to ensure proper configuration with your shipping setup.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Revize work with carrier-calculated rates?" icon="truck-fast">
    Yes. Revize fully supports carrier-calculated shipping rates. If you’d like us to help configure them for your setup, just reach out to our team at hello@revize.app.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Revize support split shipping?" icon="boxes-packing">
    It does. Revize can handle split shipping so orders with multiple items can be shipped from different locations when needed. If you’d like this enabled for your store, drop us a line at hello@revize.app.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
