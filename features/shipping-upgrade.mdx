## How customers upgrade shipping

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access shipping options">
    From the Customer Portal, customer clicks **Upgrade shipping** to see available faster delivery methods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare delivery options">
    View all available shipping methods with delivery dates, costs, and service descriptions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select faster method">
    Choose express, overnight, or other expedited options with clear delivery promises.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay the difference">
    Click **Pay now** to charge the additional shipping cost and confirm the upgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setup and configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable shipping upgrades">
    In **Order Editing** settings, turn on **Shipping upgrade** under Customer edit options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure available methods">
    Choose which shipping services customers can upgrade to based on your carrier relationships.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set upgrade rules">
    Define cutoff times, geographic restrictions, and product compatibility for each shipping method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test delivery promises">
    Verify that delivery date calculations are accurate for each shipping option and location.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Shipping upgrade best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Clear cutoff times" icon="clock">
    **When upgrades are available**
    
    Set clear deadlines for shipping upgrades:
    - "Express shipping available until 2 PM EST"
    - "Next-day shipping must be selected within 1 hour"
    - "Weekend delivery cutoff: Friday 10 AM"
    
    Prevents impossible delivery promises and customer disappointment.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Geographic accuracy" icon="globe">
    **Location-based availability**
    
    Only show upgrades that actually work:
    - Rural areas: Focus on available express options
    - Urban centers: Include same-day and weekend delivery
    - International: Show realistic expedited options
    - Weather-prone areas: Include delay disclaimers
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Product compatibility" icon="box">
    **What can ship faster**
    
    Some products can't be expedited:
    - Oversized items may exclude overnight shipping
    - Hazardous materials have carrier restrictions
    - Made-to-order items need production time
    - International shipments have customs delays
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Pricing strategy" icon="dollar-sign">
    **Upgrade pricing that works**
    
    - Price upgrades competitively but profitably
    - Consider customer's urgency level
    - Factor in your actual carrier costs
    - Build in margin for handling and risk
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
