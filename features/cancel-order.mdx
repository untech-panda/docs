</Info>

## How customers cancel orders

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access cancellation option">
    From the Customer Portal, customer clicks **Cancel order** to start the cancellation process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review cancellation details">
    See refund amount, refund method (original payment or store credit), and timing expectations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm cancellation">
    Click **Cancel order** to confirm. Customer cannot undo this action once confirmed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive refund">
    Refund processes automatically according to your [payment settings](/setup/payments-and-refunds), with confirmation notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancellation timing options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Within Edit Window">
    **Cancellations allowed during editing period**
    
    - Same timing as other order edits
    - Usually 15 minutes to few hours after checkout
    - Prevents cancellation after fulfillment starts
    
    *Most common and safest approach*
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Until Fulfillment">
    **Cancel anytime before shipping**
    
    - Available until order status changes to "Fulfilled"
    - Requires tight integration with fulfillment process
    - Higher risk of canceling orders already being picked
    
    *For advanced fulfillment workflows*
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Custom Deadlines">
    **Specific cancellation cutoffs**
    
    - Different timing from other edits
    - Matches your pick/pack schedule
    - Can be shorter or longer than edit window
    
    *Tailored to your operations*
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="No Cancellations">
    **Cancellations not allowed**
    
    - All sales final after checkout
    - Customers must contact support for special cases
    - Reduces operational complexity
    
    *For very fast fulfillment or special products*
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Refund options

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Original payment method" icon="credit-card">
    **Full refund to original payment**
    
    - Credit card, PayPal, or other payment method used at checkout
    - Refund processing takes 3-5 business days typically
    - Customer receives full order amount including tax
    - Shipping costs may be refunded or retained based on your policy
    
    **Best for:** Building customer trust and handling genuine mistakes
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Store credit" icon="gift-card">
    **Refund as store credit or gift card**
    
    - Instant refund value available immediately
    - Keeps revenue within your business
    - May offer bonus percentage for choosing store credit
    - Works well for repeat customers
    
    **Best for:** Encouraging future purchases and retaining revenue
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Partial refunds" icon="percentage">
    **Refund minus restocking or processing fees**
    
    - Deduct fixed fee or percentage from refund
    - Covers payment processing costs and handling
    - Clear fee disclosure before customer confirms cancellation
    - Helps offset operational costs
    
    **Best for:** High-volume businesses with tight margins
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No refund" icon="ban">
    **Cancellation without refund (store credit only)**
    
    - Customer cancels but keeps value as store credit
    - Prevents financial loss from cancellations
    - Must be clearly disclosed at checkout and during cancellation
    - May reduce customer satisfaction
    
    **Best for:** Specialty products or very fast fulfillment scenarios**
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Cancellation alternatives

Before customers cancel completely, consider offering:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Order Changes">
    **Fix the underlying issue**
    
    - "Need different size? Swap it instead of canceling"
    - "Wrong color? Change it in one click"
    - "Too expensive? Remove some items instead"
    
    *Turn cancellations into edits*
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Shipping Adjustments">
    **Address delivery concerns**
    
    - "Need it faster? Upgrade to express shipping"
    - "Changed address? Update shipping destination"
    - "Delivery timing issue? Schedule a better date"
    
    *Solve logistics problems*
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Discounts or Credits">
    **Provide financial incentives**
    
    - "Stay with your order, get 10% off"
    - "Free shipping upgrade instead of canceling"
    - "Store credit bonus for keeping your order"
    
    *Add value to retain the sale*
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Support Contact">
    **Human intervention option**
    
    - "Need help with something specific? Contact support"
    - "Special circumstances? Let us help you"
    - "Questions about your order? Chat with us"
    
    *Bridge to personal service*
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Operational considerations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Fulfillment integration" icon="truck">
    **Prevent shipping canceled orders**
    
    Canceled orders must immediately:
    - Remove from pick lists and fulfillment queues
    - Update inventory allocation
    - Notify 3PL or warehouse management system
    - Stop any automated shipping processes
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Inventory management" icon="boxes">
    **Return items to available stock**
    
    When orders are canceled:
    - Products return to sellable inventory immediately
    - Inventory counts update across all sales channels
    - Reserved stock allocations are released
    - Back-in-stock notifications can be triggered
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payment processing" icon="credit-card">
    **Handle refunds properly**
    
    Refund processing involves:
    - Immediate authorization void (if within window)
    - Standard refund processing (takes 3-5 days)
    - Payment gateway fee implications
    - Store credit issuance for alternative refunds
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Customer communication" icon="envelope">
    **Clear status updates**
    
    Customers should receive:
    - Instant cancellation confirmation
    - Refund processing notifications
    - Expected refund timing
    - Order status page updates
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Configure cancellation settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable order cancellation">
    In **Order Editing** settings, turn on **Cancel order** under Customer edit options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set cancellation timing">
    Choose when cancellations are allowed - same as edit window, until fulfillment, or custom timing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure refund method">
    Select original payment, store credit, partial refund, or no refund based on your business model.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add reason tracking">
    Optionally collect cancellation reasons to understand patterns and improve your business.
  </Step>
</Steps>
