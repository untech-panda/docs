## What is Revize?

Revize is a powerful Shopify app that enables customers to modify their orders after checkout, reducing support tickets while building trust through self-service options. Perfect for merchants who want to offer flexible post-purchase editing capabilities.

## What Customers Can Edit

<Info>
Orders are automatically put "on hold" after placement, giving customers time to make necessary changes.
</Info>

- **Shipping Addresses** - Update delivery locations
- **Product Details** - Change variants, colors, sizes
- **Quantities** - Adjust item quantities up or down  
- **Add Items** - Include additional products to existing orders
- **Remove Items** - Delete unwanted items from orders
