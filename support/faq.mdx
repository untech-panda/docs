## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="What is Revize?" icon="question">
Revize is a Shopify app that allows customers to edit their orders after checkout. Customers can update shipping addresses, change product quantities, swap variants, add items, or remove items during a configurable time window.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does Revize reduce customer support?" icon="headset">
By allowing customers to make changes themselves, Revize eliminates the need for customers to contact support for common order modifications. This can reduce support tickets by 30-50% for stores with high volumes of order change requests.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Revize compatible with my theme?" icon="palette">
Yes! Revize works with all standard Shopify themes and most custom themes. The editing interface is responsive and automatically adapts to your store's branding. For highly customized themes, minor adjustments may be needed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What Shopify plans does Revize support?" icon="shopify">
Revize works with all Shopify plans including Basic Shopify, Shopify, Advanced Shopify, and Shopify Plus. All features are available across all plan types.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Setup & Configuration

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How long does installation take?" icon="clock">
Installation typically takes less than 5 minutes. The app installs automatically from the Shopify App Store, and the initial setup wizard guides you through basic configuration in just a few steps.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's the default edit time limit?" icon="stopwatch">
The default edit window is 30 minutes after order placement. You can configure this anywhere from 5 minutes to 24 hours based on your business needs. Different time limits can be set for different customer segments or product categories.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I customize which orders allow editing?" icon="filter">
Yes! You can configure editing availability based on:
- Order value thresholds
- Product categories
- Customer groups
- Geographic regions
- Payment methods
- Order tags
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I test Revize before going live?" icon="flask">
Use Shopify's draft order feature to create test orders and verify the editing experience. This allows you to test all functionality without affecting real customers or inventory. See our [testing guide](/testing/draft-orders) for detailed instructions.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customer Experience

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How do customers access order editing?" icon="mouse-pointer">
Customers can edit orders through three methods:
1. **Email link** - Included in order confirmation emails
2. **Customer account** - Available in their account portal
3. **Thank you page** - Shown immediately after checkout

All methods include clear time limits and easy-to-use interfaces.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if a customer tries to edit after the time limit?" icon="clock-rotate-left">
After the edit window expires, customers see a message explaining that editing is no longer available. They're provided with contact information for your support team if they need assistance with changes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can customers see their edit history?" icon="history">
Yes! Customers can view a complete history of their order modifications in their account portal, including timestamps, changes made, and payment adjustments.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if payment fails during editing?" icon="credit-card-front">
If payment processing fails for order increases, customers are immediately notified and can:
- Try the payment again
- Use a different payment method
- Contact support for assistance
The order remains on hold until payment is resolved.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Business Operations

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How are refunds processed?" icon="money-bill-wave">
Refunds for removed items or quantity decreases are processed automatically to the original payment method. Processing time is typically:
- **Credit cards**: 3-5 business days
- **PayPal**: 2-3 business days
- **Shop Pay**: 1-2 business days
- **Other methods**: Varies by provider
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What about inventory management?" icon="boxes">
Revize integrates with Shopify's inventory system to:
- Reserve items during edit windows
- Update stock levels in real-time
- Prevent overselling
- Sync with inventory management apps
- Release reservations when windows expire
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does Revize affect my fulfillment process?" icon="truck">
Orders are automatically placed "on hold" during edit windows and released to fulfillment when editing expires. This integrates seamlessly with:
- **3PL providers** (ShipBob, Fulfillment by Amazon)
- **Shipping apps** (ShipStation, Easyship)
- **ERP systems** (NetSuite, SAP)
- **Order management** systems
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I see analytics on order editing?" icon="chart-line">
Yes! The Revize dashboard provides detailed analytics including:
- Edit completion rates
- Most common modification types
- Revenue impact from upsells
- Customer satisfaction metrics
- Support ticket reduction
- Performance by time periods
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

- **Starter**: Up to 100 orders/month
- **Growth**: Up to 1,000 orders/month  
- **Scale**: Up to 10,000 orders/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume stores

If you cancel your Revize subscription:

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the Revize dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated automatically.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Customers aren't seeing edit links" icon="link-slash">
**Common causes and solutions:**
1. **Email delivery**: Check spam folders, verify email templates
2. **Time limits**: Confirm edit window hasn't expired
3. **App settings**: Verify Revize is enabled for the order type
4. **Theme integration**: Check if theme customizations are needed

Contact support at [support@revize.app](mailto:support@revize.app) if issues persist.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Edit page won't load" icon="exclamation-triangle">
**Troubleshooting steps:**
1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser or device
3. Disable browser extensions/ad blockers
4. Check internet connection stability
5. Verify the edit link hasn't expired

If problems continue, our support team can investigate server-side issues.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Payment processing errors" icon="credit-card">
**Common payment issues:**
- **Declined cards**: Customer should contact their bank
- **Expired cards**: Customer needs to update payment method
- **Insufficient funds**: Customer should use different payment method
- **Processing errors**: Usually resolve with retry or different method

All payment errors include clear customer instructions and support contact information.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
