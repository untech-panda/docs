## How to request a refund

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reach out">
    Email us at hello@revize.app or message us in the in-app chat. Please include your account details and, if possible, a short note on why you’re requesting a refund. It helps us learn and improve.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & processing">
    Our team will review your request with care. If approved, refunds are processed through Shopify’s billing system and appear directly on your Shopify invoice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Timeline">
    Once approved, your refund will be issued within 3-5 business days back to your original payment method.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's covered

- **New subscriptions only** – Refunds apply to first payments or renewals within 30 days  
- **Full refund** – 100% of your subscription fee returned  
- **Keep your data** – Your settings and analytics remain intact even if you move to the free plan  

## What's not covered

- Subscriptions older than 30 days  
- Multiple refund requests (abuse prevention)  
- Usage fees from third-party services
