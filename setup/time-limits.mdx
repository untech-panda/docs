## Overview

Time limits control how long customers have to edit their orders after checkout. The right balance ensures customers have enough flexibility while maintaining efficient fulfillment operations.

<Info>
**Default Setting**: 30 minutes after order placement. This works well for most stores but can be customized based on your specific needs.
</Info>

## Recommended Time Limits by Business Type

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Fashion & Apparel">
**Recommended**: 1-2 hours
- Customers often need time to consult others
- Size/color changes are common
- Seasonal considerations for gift purchases
- Higher likelihood of buyer's remorse
</Tab>

<Tab title="Electronics">
**Recommended**: 15-30 minutes  
- Fast fulfillment expectations
- Technical specifications rarely change
- Lower chance of modification needs
- Quick inventory turnover
</Tab>

<Tab title="Home & Garden">
**Recommended**: 2-4 hours
- Large purchase decisions
- Measurement verification needs
- Color coordination considerations
- Seasonal project planning
</Tab>

<Tab title="Gifts & Personalized">
**Recommended**: 30 minutes - 1 hour
- Personalization errors need correction
- Shipping address changes for gifts
- Quick decision turnaround needed
- Prevent production delays
</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Configuration Options

### Basic Time Settings

<Steps>
<Step title="Access Time Settings">
1. Open your Revize dashboard
2. Navigate to **Settings** â†’ **Edit Windows**
3. Find the **Default Time Limit** section
</Step>

<Step title="Set Global Default">
Configure the default time limit that applies to all orders:
- **Minimum**: 5 minutes
- **Maximum**: 24 hours  
- **Recommended**: 30 minutes - 2 hours
- **Format**: Minutes or hours
</Step>

<Step title="Test Your Settings">
Create a draft order to verify the time limit works as expected before applying to live orders.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Advanced Time Rules

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Customer Segment Rules" icon="users">
Set different time limits based on customer characteristics:

**VIP Customers**: Extended time (up to 4 hours)
- Loyalty program members
- High-value customers  
- Repeat buyers

**New Customers**: Standard time (30 minutes)
- First-time buyers
- Unverified accounts
- Risk management

**Wholesale Customers**: Extended time (24 hours)
- Business accounts
- Bulk orders
- Complex approval processes
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Product Category Rules" icon="tags">
Different products may need different edit windows:

**Custom/Personalized Items**: Short window (15 minutes)
- Prevents production delays
- Reduces waste from changes
- Clear expectations

**Standard Products**: Standard window (30 minutes)
- Regular inventory items
- Quick fulfillment

**Pre-order Items**: Extended window (up to 24 hours)
- No immediate fulfillment pressure
- More flexibility for customers
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Order Value Rules" icon="dollar-sign">
Adjust time limits based on order amount:

**High-Value Orders** ($500+): Extended time (2-4 hours)
- More consideration time
- Reduced buyer's remorse
- Better customer experience

**Standard Orders**: Normal time (30 minutes)
- Efficient processing
- Quick turnaround

**Small Orders** (under $50): Shorter time (15 minutes)
- Fast fulfillment
- Minimal complexity
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Geographic Rules" icon="globe">
Time zones and regions may affect optimal windows:

**International Orders**: Extended time (2-4 hours)
- Time zone considerations
- Complex shipping calculations
- Currency conversion time

**Domestic Orders**: Standard time (30 minutes)
- Familiar shipping options
- Local time zones

**Express Shipping**: Reduced time (15 minutes)
- Customer expects speed
- Quick decision needed
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Time Zone Considerations

<Warning>
Ensure your time limits account for customer time zones to provide consistent experience globally.
</Warning>

### Configuration Options

**Store Time Zone** (Default)
- Uses your store's configured time zone
- Simple for single-region stores
- May confuse international customers

**Customer Time Zone** (Recommended)
- Automatically detects customer location
- Provides consistent experience
- Better for international stores

**UTC/GMT** (Advanced)
- Universal time standard
- Good for global operations
- Requires customer education

## Dynamic Time Extensions

### Automatic Extensions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Technical Issues" icon="exclamation-triangle">
**When to extend automatically:**
- Payment processing delays
- Website performance issues
- Third-party service outages
- API timeouts or errors

**Extension length**: Usually 15-30 minutes
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Inventory Conflicts" icon="boxes">
**When items become unavailable:**
- Out of stock situations
- Variant unavailability  
- Shipping restrictions discovered
- Price changes occurring

**Extension length**: Time needed to resolve issue
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Manual Extensions

Store administrators can manually extend edit windows for specific situations:
- Customer service requests
- Special circumstances
- VIP customer accommodation  
- Error corrections needed

### Seasonal Adjustments

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Holiday Seasons" icon="gift">
**Recommended adjustments:**
- **Black Friday/Cyber Monday**: Reduce to 15 minutes (high volume)
- **Christmas period**: Extend to 2-4 hours (gift considerations)
- **Valentine's Day**: Extend to 1-2 hours (last-minute changes)
- **Back-to-school**: Standard 30 minutes (quick decisions)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sales Events" icon="percent">
**Flash sales**: 5-15 minutes
- Creates urgency
- Prevents inventory issues
- Maintains sale integrity

**Clearance events**: 30 minutes - 1 hour
- Standard processing time
- Allow consideration for final sales
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
